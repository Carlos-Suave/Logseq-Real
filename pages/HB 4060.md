title: HB 4060
---

- [[Microenterprise]] development bill
	 - Microenterprises are businesses with 5 or fewer employees and include startup, home based, and self-employed individuals. 

	 - Microenterprise developmental organizations provide loans, training, and assistance to microenterprises.

	 - Basically creates a tax exempt organization dedicated to  providing loans, training and assistance to microenterprise businesses.
		 - Loan cannot exceed 50% of operating costs. 

		 - Up to 35k

		 - 50% of loans cannot exceed 10k
